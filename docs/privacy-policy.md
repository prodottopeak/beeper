# Beeper Privacy Policy

  Last updated: March 27, 2026

  Beeper ("we", "our", or "us") provides an interval chime and recurring timer app for focus, routines, and mindfulness. This Privacy Policy explains how Beeper handles
  information when you use the app.

  ## Summary

  - Beeper does not require account creation.
  - Beeper does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  - Beeper does not track you across apps or websites.
  - Most app data is stored locally on your device.
  - If you purchase Beeper Premium, the payment is processed by Apple, not by us.

  ## Information We Collect

  Beeper is designed to work primarily on-device. We do not collect personal information for our own analytics, advertising, or profiling.

  The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  - your timer, schedule, and sound preferences
  - mute and notification settings
  - onboarding and language preferences
  - local app state needed to operate features such as the next scheduled cue

  This information is stored on your device and is not used by us to identify you.

  ## Notifications and Alarms

  Beeper can request permission to send notifications and alarms so interval chimes can be delivered at the times you configure.

  Notification and alarm scheduling data is used only to provide the app's core functionality. This data is managed on your device through Apple's system frameworks.

  ## In-App Purchases

  Beeper offers an optional one-time in-app purchase, Beeper Premium.

  If you make a purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card details or banking information. We may receive limited
  transaction status information from Apple that is necessary to unlock premium features and restore purchases.

  ## Audio and Sound Previews

  Beeper may play audio previews and notification sounds that you choose inside the app. These audio selections and playback preferences are handled on-device.

  ## Data Sharing

  We do not sell your personal information.

  We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing.

  We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, ad networks, or cross-app tracking technologies in the app.

  ## Data Retention

  Because Beeper primarily stores data locally on your device, your information remains on your device unless you delete the app or clear app data through your device settings
  or backups.

  Purchase records related to App Store transactions are handled by Apple under Apple's policies.

  ## Your Choices

  You can:

  - disable notifications in iOS Settings
  - change or delete your schedules and preferences inside the app
  - stop using the app at any time
  - remove the app from your device

  ## Children's Privacy

  Beeper is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  ## International Users

  If you use Beeper outside your home country or region, your use of the App Store and related Apple services is governed by Apple's own systems and policies.

  ## Changes to This Privacy Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
